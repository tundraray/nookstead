name: Nookstead Strategic Pivot (March 2026)
description: Key insight from product audit -- AI NPCs are not the product, they're what makes the product magical. The product is a cozy life sim. NPC reactions to player actions are the core USP.
type: project
---

Nookstead has pivoted from "farming RPG with AI NPCs" to "cozy life sim where the world actually notices what you do."

**Why:** Product audit (March 2026) revealed that AI NPCs alone (Character.AI-style) lack depth, and farming alone (SDV-style) lacks uniqueness. The intersection -- NPCs that react to farming/trading/building actions -- is the true USP.

**How to apply:** Every design decision must pass the test: "Does this help the player feel that the world notices them?" Farming is context for NPC reactions, not an end in itself. NPC-NPC gossip about player actions is a key viral mechanic. The project language for the new positioning is Russian: "Уютная life sim, где мир по-настоящему замечает, что ты делаешь."

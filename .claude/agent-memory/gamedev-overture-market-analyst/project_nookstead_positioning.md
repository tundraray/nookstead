Nookstead's revised positioning (March 2026): "Уютная life sim, где мир по-настоящему замечает, что ты делаешь"

**Why:** The old positioning ("cozy browser-based farming RPG MMO с AI NPC") led with technology. The new one leads with emotional experience. Players don't want "AI NPC" — they want a world that remembers them. AI is the how, not the what.

**How to apply:** All market analysis, competitive positioning, and GTM recommendations should frame AI as invisible infrastructure, not as the headline feature. Compare with Character.AI for market validation but position against Stardew Valley / Animal Crossing for player acquisition. The monetization model is freemium with subscription (Nookstead Plus $7.99/mo) to cover ongoing LLM costs.
